About Us

Woofy Express is proud to be the slowest delivery service in the United States. Our fleet of specially trained, extremely relaxed dogs ensures your package arrives... eventually. We specialize in deliveries that take weeks, months, or even years – perfect for those who hate instant gratification!

Founded in 2025, we're revolutionizing the logistics industry by going against the grain. No drones, no next-day shipping – just pure, unadulterated slowness.
